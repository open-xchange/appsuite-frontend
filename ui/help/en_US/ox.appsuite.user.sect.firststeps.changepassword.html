3.4. Changing the password

How to change your password:

  1. Click on Portal in the menu bar

  2. Click on My password in the User data portal square.

  3. Enter the current password. Enter the new password twice.

  4. Click on Change password.

Note: This square might not be displayed. In this case you can add the square.
