3.3. Changing Personal Contact Data

How to change your personal contact data:

  1. Click the System menu icon on the right side of the menu bar. In the menu click on Settings.

  2. In the sidebar click on My contact data.

  3. Change the settings. Click on Save.

Tip: You can also change your personal contact data by using the User data portal square in the Portal application
