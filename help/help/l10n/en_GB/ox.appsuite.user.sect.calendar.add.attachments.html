7.4.8. Adding attachments

How to add attachments in the appointment editing window:

  1. Click on Add attachments below Attachments.

  2. Select at least one file.

    In order to remove an attachment, click the Delete attachment icon .

Tip: You can also add an attachment by dragging and dropping a document from a file browser or from the desktop to the appointments window.
