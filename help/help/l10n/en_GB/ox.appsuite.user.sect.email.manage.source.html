5.6.6. Showing the email source

The email source contains the complete content of an email i.e.: the complete email header data.

How to display the email source:

  1. Select an E-Mail.

  2. Click the More actions icon in the tool bar or in the detail view. Select View source.

    You can also use the context menu in the E-Mail list.
