9.2.2. Displaying email attachments in Drive

The attachments of E-Mail messages that have been sent or received by you, are displayed in a separate folder.

How to display E-Mail attachments:

  1. In the Drive app, open the My shares folder.

  2. To view an attachment's content, use the methods for viewing files.

    To display the E-Mail that belongs to the selected attachment, click on View in the tool bar. Enable File details. Click on View message.
