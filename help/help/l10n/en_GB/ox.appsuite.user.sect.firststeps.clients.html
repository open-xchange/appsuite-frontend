3.7. Manually Installing Local Apps

You can manually install local Drive apps for MS Windows, macOS, iOS or Android. The local Drive clients synchronise your Drive app data with your local workstation or mobile device.

Note: Depending on the configuration, those functions might not be available. In this case you can use a wizard to set up the apps.

How to install local Drive apps for workstations or mobile devices:

  1. Click the Settings icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click the Settings menu item.

  2. In the side bar, click on Downloads.

    Click the respective icon in the display area. Follow the instructions on installing the apps.
