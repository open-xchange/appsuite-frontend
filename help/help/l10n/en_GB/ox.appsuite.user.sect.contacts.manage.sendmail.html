6.10.1. Sending Email Messages from within an address book

You can send an E-Mail from within your address book to contacts or to a distribution list.

How to send an Email from within an address book:

  1. Select a single contact, multiple contacts or distribution lists from the list.

  2. Click on Send e-mail in the tool bar.

    If you have selected an individual contact, you can also click the Email icon in the detail view.

  3. Fill in the details for sending the E-Mail.
