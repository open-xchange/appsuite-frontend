Chapter 12. Encrypting Data with Guard

Learn how to encrypt your email messages and files with Guard.

  • Encrypt your E-Mail communication with other users or external partners.

  • Encrypt your files. Share the encrypted data with other users.

  • Use the security options to define the encryption level.

  • The encrypted data are password-protected. Depending on the configuration, you can reset your password if forgotten.

The following options are available:

  • Setting up Guard

  • Encrypting E-Mail Conversations

  • Encrypting E-Mail Conversations with Autocrypt

  • Encrypting or Decrypting Files

  • Encrypting Office documents

  • Signing Out from Guard

  • Guard Settings

Information on additional security-related topics can be found in Data Security.
