4.2.3. Adding Portal widgets

You can extend the existing widgets by adding new ones.

How to add new widgets:

  1. Use one of the following methods:

    • Click the Settings icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click on Settings.

      Click on Portal in the side bar. Click on Add widget. Select an entry.

    • Click on Add widget on the upper right side of the Portal app. Select an entry.

  2. Some widgets require additional data. Enter the required values.

User interface:

  • Add widget button
