5.1.2. The E-Mail folder view

Displays the E-Mail folders.

In order to display the folder view, click on View in the tool bar. Enable Folder view.

Content

  • Inbox. By default, all incoming E-Mail messages go to this folder.

  • Unread. This folder shows all unread messages.

  • Drafts. Contains the E-Mail messages saved as drafts.

  • Sent objects. Contains the email sent by you.

  • Trash. Contains the E-Mail messages deleted by you.

  • Archive. Contains the E-Mail messages archived by you.

  • Email. Contains the email folders created by you.

  • View all attachments. Shows all email attachments that you have sent or received, in the Drive app.

  • Add email account. Opens a window for adding additional email accounts.

Depending on the configuration, additional folders might be available:

  • Shared email folders you subscribed to.

  • Folders from functional email accounts that have been assigned to you by the administrator.
