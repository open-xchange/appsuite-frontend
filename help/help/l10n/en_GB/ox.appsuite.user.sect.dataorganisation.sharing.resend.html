10.2.3. Resending an invitation

You can resend an invitation to an already granted share.

How to resend an invitation to a person:

  1. In the folder view, select a folder that you shared.

  2. Click the Actions icon next to the folder name. Click on Share / Permissions. Click on Permissions in the E-Mail app.

    In the Drive app, you can also click on Share in the tool bar.

    The window for granting permissions opens.

  3. Click the Actions icon next to a user name. Click on Resend invitation.

  4. Click on Save.
