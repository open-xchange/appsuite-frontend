6.6.2. Saving E-Mail recipients as distribution list

If an E-Mail contains multiple recipients, you can save all the recipients as a new distribution list.

How to save the recipients of an E-Mail as a distribution list:

  1. Select an E-Mail in the E-Mail app.

  2. Click the More actions icon in the detail view.

    Click on Save as distribution list.

  3. Complete the details for creating the distribution list.
