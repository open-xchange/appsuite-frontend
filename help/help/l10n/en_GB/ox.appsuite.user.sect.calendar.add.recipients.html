7.4.12. Inviting E-Mail recipients to new appointments

If there are several E-Mail recipients, all recipients can be invited to a new appointment.

How to invite all E-Mail recipients to an appointment:

  1. Select an E-Mail in the E-Mail app.

  2. Click the More actions iconin the detail view.

    Click on Invite to appointment.

  3. Complete the details for creating an appointment.
