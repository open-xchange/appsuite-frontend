6.10.5. Adding contacts as XING contacts

You can add a contact as an XING contact if you are not connected with this contact in XING. The contact and you need to have XING accounts.

How to add a contact as an XING contact:

  1. Select a contact from the list.

  2. Click the More actions icon in the tool bar. Click on Add to Xing.
