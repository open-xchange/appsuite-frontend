10.2.6. Subscribing to shared or personal E-Mail folders

In order to see E-Mail folders shared by other users, you have to subscribe to those folders. You are automatically subscribed to your personal E-Mail folders. To hide a personal E-Mail folder, you have to cancel the subscription.

How to subscribe to personal or shared E-Mail folders:

  1. Click the Settings icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click on Settings.

  2. Click on E-Mail in the side bar.

  3. Click on Change IMAP subscription in the display area.

  4. In the Subscribe IMAP folders window activate the checkboxes of the folders that you want to subscribe to. Click on Save.
