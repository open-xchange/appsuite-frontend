7.7.3. Managing favourite timezones

Timezones marked as favourites by you, can be displayed in the calendar sheet in addition to the preset timezone.

How to mark a timezone as favourite:

  1. Click the Settings icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click the Settings menu item.

  2. Select Calendar from the side bar. Click on Favourite timezones.

  3. Click on Add timezone in the display area. The Select favourite timezone window opens.

  4. Select a timezone from the list. Click on Add.

To remove a timezone from the list of favorites, click the Delete icon next to the timezone.
