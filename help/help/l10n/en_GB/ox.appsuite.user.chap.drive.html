Chapter 9.  Drive

Learn how to use the Drive app to save and view files, documents and other media and use them together with other people.

How to start the Drive app:

  • Click the All Applications icon in the menu bar. Click on Drive in the App Launcher.

    Depending on the configuration, you can also click the quick start icon in the menu bar for launching the app. Drive
