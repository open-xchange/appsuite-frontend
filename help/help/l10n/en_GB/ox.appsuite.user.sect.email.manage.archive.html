5.5.13. Archiving E-Mail messages

You can archive a folder's E-Mail messages that are older than 90 days. The E-Mail messages are moved to the Archive folder. The Archive folder contains a separate subfolder for each calendar year. The archived E-Mail messages are saved to those subfolders sorted by the year of receipt. The Archive folder is created as soon as you initially use this function.

How to archive an E-Mail folder's content:

  1. Select an email folder in the folder tree.

  2. Click the Folder-specific actions icon next to the folder name. Click on Archive.

  3. In the Archive messages window click on Archive.

Note: You might have to refresh the page in order to display the folder.
