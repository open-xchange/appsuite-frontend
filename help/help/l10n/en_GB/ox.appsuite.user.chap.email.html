Chapter 5.  E-Mail

Learn how to read, send and organise your email message in the E-Mail app.

How to start the E-Mail app:

  • Click the All Applications icon in the menu bar. Click on E-Mail in the App Launcher.

    Depending on the configuration, you can also click the quick start icon in the menu bar for launching the E-Mail app.
