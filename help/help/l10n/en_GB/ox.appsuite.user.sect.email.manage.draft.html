Managing E-Mail messages

5.7.7. Using E-Mail drafts

While composing an E-Mail, the E-Mail is automatically saved as an E-Mail draft in regular intervals. The following options are available:

  • Edit or send an E-Mail draft.

  • Edit or send a copy of an E-Mail draft.

The E-Mail draft will be deleted after it has been sent. To keep the E-Mail draft, you have to send a copy.

How to use an E-Mail draft:

  1. Select an E-Mail in the Drafts folder.

  2. Click on Edit draft or on Edit copy in the tool bar.

    Edit the content.

  3. In order to finish editing, click on Cancel or the Close icon . Click on Save as draft in the next window.

    To send the draft or the draft's copy, click on Send.
