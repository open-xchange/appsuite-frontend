5.7.12. Printing email messages

You can print the content of an E-Mail or several E-Mail messages.

How to print an E-Mail's content:

  1. Select one or several E-Mail messages.

  2. Click the More actions icon in the tool bar or in the detail view. Click on Print in the menu.

    You can also use the context menu in the E-Mail list.

    A window with a print preview opens.

  3. If required, change the printer settings. Click on the Print button.
