6.10.4. Sending contacts as vCard

You can send contacts or distribution lists as vCard attachment to an E-Mail.

How to send a contact as a vCard attachment:

  1. Select a single contact or distribution list, or multiple contacts or distribution lists from the list.

  2. Click the More actions icon in the tool bar. Click on Send as vCard.

  3. Fill in the details for sending the E-Mail.
