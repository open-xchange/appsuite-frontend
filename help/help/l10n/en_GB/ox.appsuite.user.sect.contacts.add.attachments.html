6.5.2. Adding attachments

How to add attachments in the contact editing window:

  1. Click on Add attachment at the bottom.

  2. Select one or several files.

    In order to remove an attachment, click on Remove attachment next to the attachment.

Tip: You can also add an attachment by dragging and dropping a document from a file browser or from the desktop to the contacts window.
