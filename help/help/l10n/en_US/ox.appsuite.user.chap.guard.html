Chapter 13. Encrypting Data with Guard

Guard is a groupware security component that allows to encrypt E-Mails and files.

  • Encrypt your E-Mail communication with other users or external partners.

  • Encrypt your files. Share the encrypted data with other users.

  • Use the security options to define the encryption level.

  • The encrypted data are password-protected. Depending on the configuration, you can reset your password if forgotten.
