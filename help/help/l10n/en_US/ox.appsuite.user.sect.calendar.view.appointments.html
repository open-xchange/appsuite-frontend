Viewing Appointments

7.2.3. How are appointments displayed?

In a calendar view, appointments are displayed in the following colors, depending on the availability:

  • Tentative: yellow

  • Free: green

  • Booked: blue

  • Absent: red

Private appointments are always displayed in gray.
