Data Organization and Shares

10.3. Appointing a Deputy

You can appoint other users to act as your deputy. You can grant one or multiple deputies different permission levels to handle the email communication and appointment scheduling in your name.

The following options exist:

  • Adding deputies

  • Viewing, editing, deleting deputies
