12.6. Signing Out from Guard

You can sign out from Guard without closing the groupware. To open an encrypted E-Mail or file after having signed out, you have to re-enter the Guard security password.

Note: This function is only available if you enable Remember Password when opening an encrypted E-Mail or file.

How to sign out from Guard:

  1. Click the Support icon on the right side of the menu bar.

  2. Click on Sign out Guard.
