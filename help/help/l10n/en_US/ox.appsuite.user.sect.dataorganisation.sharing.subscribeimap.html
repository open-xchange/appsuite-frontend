10.2.6. Subscribing to shared or personal email folders

In order to see email folders shared by other users, you have to subscribe to those folders. You are automatically subscribed to your personal email folders. To hide a personal email folder, you have to cancel the subscription.

How to subscribe to personal or shared email folders:

  1. Click the Settings icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click on Settings.

  2. Click on Mail in the sidebar.

  3. Click on Change IMAP subscription in the display area.

  4. In the Subscribe IMAP folders window activate the checkboxes of the folders that you want to subscribe to. Click on Save.
