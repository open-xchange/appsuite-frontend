Appointments in teams

7.8.2. Sending an E-Mail to participants

You can send an E-Mail to all appointment participants.

How to send an E-Mail to all appointment participants:

  1. Select an appointment from the list or in a calendar view.

  2. Click on Send mail to all participants in the toolbar.

  3. Fill in the details in order to Send a new E-Mail.
