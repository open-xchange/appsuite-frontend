3.2. Changing the Regional Settings

In the regional settings, you set the data formatting and the basic calendar settings:

  • time format

  • date format

  • number format

  • first weekday of a calendar week

  • first calendar week of a year

The following options exist:

  • select a regional setting

  • customize the regional setting

How to change the regional settings:

  1. Click the Settings icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click on Settings.

  2. Click on Basic settings in the sidebar.

    Click on More regional settings in the display area. The Regional settings window opens.

  3. Change the settings.

    To undo the change, click on Reset.
