10.4. Managing Accounts

Learn how to manage your accounts that have been set up by default or have been set up by you.

  • your primary email account

  • functional email accounts, if available

  • social network accounts

  • your Google account for calendar, contacts, Drive

  • storage accounts

The following options exist:

  • Editing the primary email account

  • Disabling or enabling functional email accounts

  • Viewing, editing, deleting accounts
