11.3.2. Viewing, editing, deleting accounts

Depending on the account type, you can edit different account settings:

  • For E-Mail accounts: account name, sender name, Unified Mail usage, server parameters, folder names

  • for social networks: account name

How to view, edit or delete accounts:

  1. Click the Settings iconon the right side of the menu bar. Click on Settings.

  2. In the sidebar, click on Accounts.

  3. Click on Edit next to an account in the display area. The settings are displayed in an editing window.

    If you use several services from the selected provider, e.g. Google, the editing window shows an overview of the services.

  4. Change the settings.

    Notes for editing external E-Mail accounts:

    • To change the preset sender name, click into the input field next to Your name.

    • You can overwrite this preset name when composing an E-Mail.

Further options in the editing window:

  • Depending on the account type, you can open the respective folder in the corresponding app by clicking on the name of the service.

  • If you no longer want to access an account from within the groupware, you can delete this account.

    To do so, click the Delete icon next to an account.
