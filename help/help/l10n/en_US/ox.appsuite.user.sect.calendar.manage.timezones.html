7.6.4. Managing favorite timezones

Timezones marked as favorites by you, can be displayed in the calendar sheet in addition to the preset timezone.

How to mark a timezone as favorite:

  1. Click the System menu icon on the right side of the menu bar. Click the Settings menu item.

  2. Select Calendar from the sidebar. Click on Favorite timezones.

  3. Click on Add timezone in the display area. The Select favorite timezone window opens.

  4. Select a timezone from the list. Click on Add.

To remove a timezone from the list of favorites, click the Delete icon next to the timezone.
