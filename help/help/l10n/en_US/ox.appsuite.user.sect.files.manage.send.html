9.4.2. Sending files as E-Mail attachments

You can send the current versions of files as E-Mail attachments.

How to send files as an E-Mail attachment:

  1. Select one or several files in the display area. Click the Actions icon in the toolbar. Click on Send by mail in the menu.

    You can also use the Actions icon .

  2. On the Compose page, complete the details for sending the E-Mail.
