6.7.8. Inviting contacts to Xing

You can invite a contact to Xing if this contact has no Xing account yet.

How to invite a contact to Xing:

  1. Select a contact from the list.

  2. Click the Actions icon in the toolbar. Click the menu entry Invite to Xing.
