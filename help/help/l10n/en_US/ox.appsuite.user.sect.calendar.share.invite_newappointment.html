Appointments in teams

7.8.3. Inviting participants to a new appointment

If an appointment has several participants, you can invite those participants to a new appointment.

How to invite participants to a new appointment:

  1. Depending on the view selected, use one of the following methods:

    • Click on an appointment in a calendar view. Click on Invite to appointment in the display area.

    • Select an appointment in the list view. Click on Invite to appointment in the display area.

  2. Complete the details for creating an appointment.
